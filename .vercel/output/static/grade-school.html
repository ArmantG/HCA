ACADEMICS

Grade School

The Department

The Grade School Department provides a nurturing, Christ-centered foundation where learners build strong academic habits, develop character, and grow in confidence. Our focus includes literacy and numeracy, healthy routines, respectful relationships, and age-appropriate service. Through engaging instruction and consistent care, learners are prepared to thrive in the classroom, at home, and in their community.

Objectives

  1. Build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and learning habits.
    1. Reading fluency, comprehension, and clear communication.
    2. Number sense and problem solving through practice and play.
    3. Curiosity and confidence in asking questions and exploring new ideas.
  2. Develop character and faith-filled habits.
    1. Respect, kindness, and responsibility in daily interactions.
    2. Participation in prayer, worship, and community service.
    3. Habits of honesty, gratitude, and self-control.
  3. Promote healthy growth and safe routines.
    1. Good hygiene, healthy food choices, and active play.
    2. Social-emotional skills for confidence and resilience.
    3. A safe, caring classroom culture.
  4. Encourage service and community involvement.
    1. Helping others through age-appropriate service projects.
    2. Care for school property and the environment.
    3. Appreciation for family, community, and country.
