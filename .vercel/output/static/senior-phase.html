Senior Phase

Grade 7

Our Senior Phase focuses on Grade 7 readiness for high school. Learners deepen subject knowledge, develop study discipline, and grow into leadership roles within the school.

Curriculum Focus

  • Advanced literacy and writing across subjects
  • Mathematics, Natural Sciences, and Social Sciences depth
  • Technology, Creative Arts, and Life Skills continuation

Learning Approach

  • Higher expectations for independent work and time management
  • Research tasks and projects that build academic confidence
  • Support for transition to Grade 8 and high school routines

Assessment & Support

  • Formal tests, exams, and project-based assessment
  • Exam preparation strategies and study skills coaching
  • Regular feedback and goal-setting with learners

Leadership & Service

  • Leadership roles through class responsibilities and events
  • Service projects that build empathy and community care
  • Faith-based guidance for character and integrity
