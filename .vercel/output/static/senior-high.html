ACADEMICS

Senior High

The Department

The Senior High Department provides a well-rounded education that emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, and collaboration skills for college and career readiness. Along with academic excellence, the department prioritizes personal development by promoting healthy routines, self-discipline, and a clear sense of purpose. Learners are guided to make informed pathway choices, develop their talents, and lead with integrity. Christ- centered formation encourages service, respectful leadership, and responsibility in the wider community.

Strands Offered

Science, Technology, Engineering, & Mathematics Innovating the future through science and technology.
Accountancy, Business, & Management Shaping future business leaders and managers.
Humanities & Social Sciences Empowering critical thinking and social awareness.
Arts & Design Celebrating creativity and imagination through the arts.
Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Equipping students with practical skills for real-world success.
General Academic Strand Broadening knowledge and opportunities for further studies.

Objectives

  1. Strengthen character and values that guide daily life.
    1. Respectful communication and teamwork with peers and staff.
    2. Integrity, honesty, and responsibility in choices.
    3. Participation in chapel, service, and community life.
  2. Promote lifelong learning through self-discovery, expression, and creativity.
    1. Advanced study habits, research skills, and critical thinking.
    2. Clear communication for academic and career readiness.
    3. Exploration of talents and informed pathway choices.
  3. Promote healthy growth and personal discipline.
    1. Healthy routines, active living, and responsible decision making.
    2. Resilience, self-awareness, and emotional balance.
    3. Self-discipline and perseverance in academics and activities.
  4. Deepen commitment to service and community responsibility.
    1. Participation in service projects and outreach opportunities.
    2. Respect for others and care for the environment.
    3. Positive leadership and contribution to school life.
